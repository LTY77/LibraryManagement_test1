Library Penalty Management Regulations
Administrator: Students should check out books in accordance with the following rules
"(1) Overdue books are subject to a penalty of $0.1 per book per day.
⑵Books in the collection must not be scrawled at will. If a book is defaced, a penalty of RMB 0.5 per page will be imposed, and if the defacement is serious or exceeds 10 pages, the book will be treated as a lost book.
⑶If a book is lost, you may purchase the same edition and pay a book processing fee of $10, or you may pay 3 to 10 times the amount of compensation in accordance with the relevant library rules and regulations.
⑷Anyone stealing books will be fined at least 10 times the price of the book and the whole school will be informed and criticised, and serious cases will be referred to the school security office or public security authorities."
